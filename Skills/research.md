# Skill: Research

## Purpose
Gather, analyze, and synthesize information on specific topics to support decision-making and planning.

## Inputs
- Research topic: Subject to investigate
- Scope: Breadth and depth of research
- Sources: Preferred information sources
- Deadline: When research is needed
- Output format: Report/Summary/Briefing

## Outputs
- Research report: Structured findings
- Source list: References and citations
- Key insights: Main discoveries and patterns
- Recommendations: Actionable next steps
- Confidence level: Assessment of information quality

## Process

1. **Define Research Scope**
   - Clarify research questions
   - Identify key areas to investigate
   - Set boundaries and constraints
   - Determine success criteria

2. **Gather Information**
   - Search internal vault for existing knowledge
   - Query external sources (if MCP enabled)
   - Collect relevant data and documents
   - Note source credibility and dates

3. **Analyze Findings**
   - Identify patterns and trends
   - Compare different perspectives
   - Validate information across sources
   - Note gaps and uncertainties

4. **Synthesize Insights**
   - Connect related information
   - Draw conclusions
   - Identify implications
   - Formulate recommendations

5. **Document Results**
   - Structure findings clearly
   - Include supporting evidence
   - Cite sources properly
   - Highlight key takeaways

## Success Criteria
- Research questions fully addressed
- Multiple credible sources consulted
- Findings clearly documented
- Actionable insights provided
- Sources properly cited

## Error Handling
- **Insufficient information**: Document gaps, suggest additional sources
- **Conflicting sources**: Present multiple viewpoints, note discrepancies
- **Outdated information**: Flag date concerns, seek current data
- **Scope creep**: Refocus on original questions, note related topics for future research

## Examples

### Example 1: Technology Research
**Input:**
- Topic: "Best project management tools for small teams"
- Scope: Features, pricing, integrations
- Deadline: 3 days

**Output:**
**Research Report: Project Management Tools**

**Executive Summary:**
Analyzed 5 leading PM tools for small teams. Asana and ClickUp emerge as top choices based on features, pricing, and ease of use.

**Key Findings:**
- Asana: Best for simple workflows ($10.99/user/month)
- ClickUp: Most features for price ($5/user/month)
- Monday.com: Best UI but expensive ($12/user/month)
- Trello: Good for basic needs (Free tier available)
- Notion: Best for documentation integration ($8/user/month)

**Comparison Matrix:**
| Tool | Price | Features | Integrations | Learning Curve |
|------|-------|----------|--------------|----------------|
| Asana | $$$ | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Low |
| ClickUp | $$ | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Medium |

**Recommendation:**
Start with ClickUp for best value. Consider Asana if integrations are priority.

**Sources:**
- Official product websites (accessed 2024-02-12)
- G2 reviews (500+ reviews per product)
- Internal team feedback

### Example 2: Market Research
**Input:**
- Topic: "Competitor analysis for Product X"
- Scope: Features, pricing, market position
- Deadline: 1 week

**Output:**
**Competitor Analysis: Product X Market**

**Market Overview:**
3 major competitors identified with combined 67% market share. Market growing at 15% annually.

**Competitor Profiles:**
1. **Competitor A** (Market Leader - 35%)
   - Strengths: Brand recognition, enterprise features
   - Weaknesses: High price, complex setup
   - Pricing: $50-200/month

2. **Competitor B** (Fast Grower - 20%)
   - Strengths: Modern UI, good support
   - Weaknesses: Limited integrations
   - Pricing: $30-100/month

3. **Competitor C** (Budget Option - 12%)
   - Strengths: Low price, simple
   - Weaknesses: Fewer features
   - Pricing: $10-40/month

**Market Gaps:**
- Mid-market segment underserved
- Integration with Tool Y lacking across all competitors
- Mobile experience weak

**Strategic Recommendations:**
1. Target mid-market with $40-80 pricing
2. Prioritize Tool Y integration
3. Invest in mobile experience

**Confidence Level:** High (based on public data, user reviews, and market reports)
